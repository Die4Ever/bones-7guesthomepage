This room is all about the characters in the game. Where they came from, what their heart's most secret desire was and who played them.

We kick off with the most insane character of them all, the madman himself: mister Henry Stauf.
His mother remains unknown, but everything indicates that she was an 'unmarried working girl'. Our little crazyman was brought up in Vincent's Orphanage in Hell's kitchen (No really). Henry was impossible to handle, his violence, stealing and lying became intollerable. When Henry became an adult, he stayed at an reformatory untill one day he had enough and started wondering. He robbed stores and lived the life of a poor crook. One day a woman came home from quire practice (but she never got there). Stauf killed Grace Willet with a simple blow on the head with a hammer he had stolen once. Now he was in serious trouble. That night he was hiding in the forest, as soon as he fell asleep he had a vision, voices in his head sometimes told him to do things and now they told him to make a toy, a doll just as he saw it in his vision. Stauf obeyed, he went to a bar, Benny's cafe, in Harley, he traded the doll for food and shelter. The doll was a success and that night he had another vision, another toy he was to build. This became an even bigger hit and Stauf could give up his drifter existence. One day he opened a shop, Wonderworld and puzzles. The famous toys sold out easily and Henry became a respected member of the community.

Then a strange virus came and killed a lot of children, all of a sudden Stauf closed his shop and began to build a strange house, a mansion on a hill. After completion of the odd house Stauf broke all contact with the village, until one day. Six invitations arrived in Harley-on-the-Hudson.

The guests, driven by their curiocity and personal problems, quickly accepted. One night, they all walked up the hill and entered the house. They were never seen again. Later that night, a boy named Tad Stone entered the house. He played a game with his friends, truth or dare, and he dared to go into the house.

Then the tale of Henry turns into a mystery, nor the six guests, nor the boy, nor Henry Stauf himself were seen again. Stauf's agent said that Stauf took a world cruise and never came back, but I know better. Henry's still here, waiting, plotting, crazy, sick and mean!
