"Wanna balloon, sonny?"

These are the system requirements you need to have to play the seventh guest:

Macintosh

Required Hardware:

20 MHz or higher 68030 or 68040 based computer
System 7 or higher
4MB RAM
Hard disk with 7 MB free space
CD-ROM drive with a minimum of 150K transfer rate
CD-ROM software 4.1 or higher
Monitor capable of 256 colors

Supports:

Macintosh LCIII, IIsi, IIfx, IIvx, Performa 460 or higher, Centris 610, Centris 650, Quadra 700, Quadra 800, Quadra 660 AV, Quadra 840 AV.

Recommended Hardware:

68040 33Mhz or faster processor
8MB RAM
CD-ROM drive with 300k per second transfer rate

PC

Minimum System Requirements:

386DX-25 MHz processor minimum
2MB RAM
16-bit SVGA card with at least 512k of memory
CD-ROM drive with minimum of 150KB per second transfer rate
Sound card with FM and PCM sound
Hard drive with 10MB of free space
MSCDEX version 2.2 or higher
DOS 5.0 or higher
Microsoft compatible mouse

Supports:

Roland Midi cards MT-32, LAPC-1
Sound Canvas, SCC-1, SoundBlaster (and compatible sound cards)
SoundBlaster Pro, ProAudio Spectrum (8 and 16 bit)
Thunderboard, Adlib Gold

Recommended Hardware:

486SX-33Mhz or faster processor
4MB of RAM
16-bit General Midi compatible sound card
Fast 16-bit SVGA video card with 1 MB of memory or local bus memory
Double-speed CD-ROM drive
Roland LAPC-1, MT-32, or Sound Canvas
